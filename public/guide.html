How to Play Virtual Bingo

A complete guide for hosts and players — no app download, no account, works on any phone.

🎙 Admin (Host) 🎯 Player 📺 Spectate
🎙 Admin

Running the game as Host

1

Open the admin page

Visit /admin on your device — the one you'll use to run the game. This is your control center throughout the session.

  • If playing on a local network, use the IP address printed in the terminal: http://192.168.x.x:4590/admin
  • If deployed to Render, use your permanent URL: https://virtual-bingo-xxxx.onrender.com/admin
💡 Keep this tab open throughout the game — closing it doesn't end the session, but you'll need the join code to get back.
virtual-bingo-xxxx.onrender.com/admin

Open the lobby

Click "Open Lobby". A QR code and 6-character join code appear instantly. Share these with your players — they'll use it to join.

  • The QR code links directly to the join page with the code pre-filled — scanning is all a player needs to do
  • The join code (e.g. K3F9QX) is for players who can't scan QR — they type it at /join
  • Show the QR on a TV or projector using the ⛶ Full screen button on the QR card
💡 The QR code survives page refresh on the same browser — the join code is saved in localStorage automatically.

Watch players join in real time

As players scan the QR and enter their names, they appear on your lobby screen instantly — no refresh needed.

  • Each player's name pops in as they join
  • The player count updates live
  • The "Start Game" button activates once at least one player has joined
  • There's no maximum wait time — take as long as you need for stragglers
💡 Up to 50 players can join a single game. If someone types a name that's already taken, they'll be asked to choose a different one.

Start the game and draw numbers

Click "Start Game". All players are immediately redirected to their bingo ticket. You're now in the draw screen.

  • Click "🎉 Draw Number" to call the next ball — it's randomly selected from the remaining 75
  • The bowl animation shakes and the ball pops up with its letter prefix (e.g. N-42)
  • Every connected device updates within a second
  • The history row below shows all called numbers in order
  • The remaining count counts down from 75
💡 Use the ⛶ Full screen button on the draw card to fill a TV or projector screen with the current number — perfect for calling out loud.

Joining and playing

Scan the QR code

Point your phone camera at the QR code on the host's screen. Tap the link that appears — it opens the join page with the game code already filled in.

No QR? Visit the join URL directly and type the 6-character code when prompted:

https://virtual-bingo-xxxx.onrender.com/join
— or —
http://192.168.x.x:4590/join
💡 No app download needed. Works in Safari, Chrome, Firefox — any modern browser on any phone.

2

Enter your name

Type your name — this is how you appear on the scoreboard and leaderboard. No account, no email, just a name.

  • Names are case-insensitive for uniqueness — "Grandma" and "grandma" are treated as the same
  • If your name is already taken, you'll be asked to pick a different one
  • Max 30 characters
💡 Your name and player ID are saved in your browser's localStorage — refreshing the page brings you straight back to your ticket.

Wait in the lobby

Your bingo ticket

When the game starts, your unique 5×5 ticket appears. Every player gets a different randomly-generated card.

  • The center cell is FREE — already marked for you
  • Columns follow standard 75-ball ranges: B=1–15, I=16–30, N=31–45, G=46–60, O=61–75
  • The last called number and recent call history are shown above your ticket
  • Numbers you can mark (called but not yet marked) glow with a yellow ring

5

Tap squares to mark them

When a number on your ticket is called, tap it to mark it. The server validates every tap — you can only mark numbers that have actually been drawn.

  • Numbers with a yellow glow are called and ready to mark
  • Tap an already-marked square to un-mark it (toggle)
  • The FREE center is pre-marked and can't be toggled
  • Marked squares turn pink/purple with a checkmark
💡 Your marks are cached in localStorage — if you refresh the page, your marks are restored instantly from the cache while the server syncs.

6

Claim your prize

When you complete a line (row, column, or diagonal), a pulsing claim banner appears automatically. Tap it to claim your prize.

  • 1 Line — any complete row, column, or diagonal (first prize)
  • 2 Lines — a second complete line (can only claim after 1 Line is taken)
  • Full House — all 25 squares marked
  • The server validates your claim — if the line isn't actually complete, the claim is rejected
  • Once a tier is claimed by anyone, it's gone — the banner disappears for everyone
💡 A win celebration pops up on every player's and the admin's screen simultaneously when a prize is claimed.

📺 Spectate

Live scoreboard for TV or projector

1

Open /spectate on any screen

Visit /spectate on any device you want to use as a big-screen display — a TV, laptop on a projector, or a second monitor. No login required.

  • Shows a live ranked list of every player, sorted by progress
  • Each player card has a progress bar (squares marked out of 25), line pips, and a trophy when they win
  • The last drawn number and call history appear at the top
  • Prizes claimed so far are shown at the bottom
  • Updates in real time — same SSE stream as the player view
  • If no game is running, shows a "Waiting for game to start" placeholder
💡 The spectate page fills the full screen automatically. Open it in fullscreen mode (F11 on most browsers) for a clean TV display with no browser chrome.
